# Environment & Interaction in Unity

This chapter explores high-fidelity rendering and human-robot interaction concepts in Unity environments. You'll learn about visual realism versus physical accuracy, and how Unity complements physics simulation for comprehensive digital twin environments.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Understand Unity's role in high-fidelity rendering for robotics
- Explain human-robot interaction concepts in digital environments
- Distinguish between visual realism and physical accuracy requirements
- Apply these concepts to create effective digital twin interfaces
