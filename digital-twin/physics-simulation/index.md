# Physics Simulation with Gazebo

This chapter introduces physics simulation in digital twin environments using Gazebo. You'll learn about the fundamental concepts that make digital twins effective for robotics development and testing.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Explain the role of digital twins in robotics
- Understand how gravity, collisions, and physics engines work in simulation
- Describe the process of simulating humanoid motion in physics environments
- Apply these concepts to create realistic robotic simulations

- Basic understanding of ROS 2 concepts
- Familiarity with robotics fundamentals
